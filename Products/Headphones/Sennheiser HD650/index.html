Sennheiser HD650

a precisely tuned dynamic driver with a lightweight diaphragm, it delivers smooth frequency
response and excellent transient performance. Its open-back design enhances spatial detail and
creates a wide, natural soundstage, allowing instruments and vocals to breathe. The result is a
headphone that combines resolution and musical warmth, maintaining faithful tonal balance across
the full audio spectrum.
